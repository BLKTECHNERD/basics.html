Honey Marshall Biography

Honey Marshall was born in Los Angeles on September 11th, 1991 to the parents of Michael and Whitney Marshall. The California native attended Hollywood High Performing Arts Center where she was a member of the softball, basketball and track team as well as a member of the drama club.



Marshall studied at UCLA and graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in African American Studies in 2012. She then studied acting at the Yale School of Drama and obtained a Master of Fine Arts in 2018, despite opposition from her grandmother who warned her to not "waste" her "Yale education on theater." She was the only member of Marshall's family to have gone to both college and graduate school.



Since then, Honey Marshall has starred in full feature films such as Star, Journey To Finding Peace and Where Do We Go From Here? In 2021, Honey was honord with a star on Hollywood boulevard for her film career. She now resides in the valley of Los Angeles with her golden retriever and two cats.
